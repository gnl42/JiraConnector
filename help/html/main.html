The Mylyn Tasks Connector: Jira is an Eclipse plugin that lets you work with the Atlassian products within your IDE. Now you don't have to switch between websites, email messages and news feeds to see what's happening to your project and your code. Instead, you can see the relevant JIRA issues and Bamboo build information right there in your development environment.
